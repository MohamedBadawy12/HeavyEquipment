--⚙️ HeavyHub — Heavy Equipment Rental Platform
-🌟 Overview
HeavyHub is a full-stack ASP.NET MVC platform that connects heavy equipment owners with contractors and renters across Egypt and the Middle East. The platform handles the complete rental lifecycle — from browsing and booking, through inspection and logistics, to payment and reviews.

-Core Actors
Role                       Description
🏗 OwnerLists             equipment, manages rentals, receives earnings
🚜 Customer              Browses, books, and pays for equipment rentals
⚙️ Admin                 Manages platform, verifies users, approves transactions
